Vanderbilt lost a nail-biter to No. 8 Notre Dame, while South Carolina had the week off.
On Saturday, they will face off in an SEC clash at Vanderbilt Stadium. Kickoff will be 3 p.m. CT on SEC Network.
It will be the start of SEC play for Vanderbilt (2-1), which fell 22-17 to Notre Dame. South Carolina (1-1, 0-1 SEC) suffered a 41-17 loss to Georgia on Sept. 8, but its home game against Marshall was canceled due to Hurricane Florence.
South Carolina owns a 23-4 record in the all-time series. The Gamecocks have won nine straight over Vanderbilt.
